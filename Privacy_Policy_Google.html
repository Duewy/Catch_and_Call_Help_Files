1. Overview

Catch & Call is a fishing catch-logging and tournament-tracking app developed by BrameStorm. This Privacy Policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and what is stored. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Data We Collect

Catch & Call collects only the data you directly enter into the app:

  • Fish species, size, weight, and catch date/time
  • GPS location coordinates (Tracker and Pro VC editions only, when enabled by the user)
  • Clip colors and tournament round data (Tournament Mode)
  • App preferences and settings stored locally on your device

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or account registration information through the app
  • We do not transmit your catch data to BrameStorm servers
  • We do not sell your personal data
  • We do not share your catch logs with third parties

4. Location (GPS)

The Tracker and Pro VC editions may request access to your device location to record where fish were caught. Location data is stored locally on your device only. You can deny or disable location access through your device settings.

The Free edition does not use GPS for catch logging. Google AdMob may use approximate location derived from network or IP-based signals for ad delivery. That processing is handled by Google and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Microphone & Speech Recognition

Some editions of Catch & Call include voice-related features that may use your device microphone and speech recognition services. Audio handling and speech processing may rely on operating system services or third-party platform services available on your device.

BrameStorm does not store voice recordings as part of normal app operation and does not transmit your catch log data to BrameStorm servers.

6. Advertising

Certain editions of the app may display advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and approximate location information for ad delivery, fraud prevention, and related advertising operations. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Catch data entered into the app is stored locally on your device and is not shared with AdMob by BrameStorm.

7. Data Storage

All catch data is stored locally on your device. BrameStorm does not operate cloud backup, synchronization, or recovery services for this app. You are responsible for backing up your device and data.

8. Children's Privacy

Catch & Call is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
