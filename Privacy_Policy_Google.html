🎣 Catch & Call — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 15, 2026
Last Updated: March 15, 2026
Developer: BrameStorm

1. Overview

Catch & Call is a fishing catch-logging and tournament-tracking app developed by BrameStorm. This Privacy Policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and what is stored. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Data We Collect

Catch & Call collects only the data you directly enter into the app:

  • Fish species, size, weight, and catch date/time
  • GPS location coordinates (Tracker and Pro VC editions only, when enabled by the user)
  • Clip colors and tournament round data (Tournament Mode)
  • App preferences and settings stored locally on your device

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any account information
  • We do not transmit your catch data to any server
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs beyond Google AdMob (Base edition)

4. Location (GPS)

The Tracker and Pro VC editions may request access to your device location to record where fish were caught. Location data is stored locally on your device only. You can disable GPS at any time in the app's Settings.

The Base edition does not request or use your device GPS for catch logging. Google AdMob may use approximate location derived from your IP address to serve relevant ads — this is handled entirely by Google and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Microphone & Speech Recognition

The Tracker edition includes a Voice Practice feature that uses your device microphone and Apple's Speech Recognition framework to help you practice voice commands. Audio is processed on-device by Apple and is not recorded or stored by BrameStorm.

The Pro VC edition uses your microphone and speech recognition for full hands-free catch logging. Again, audio processing is handled on-device by Apple frameworks and is not stored or transmitted by BrameStorm.

6. Advertising (Base Edition Only)

The free Base edition displays banner advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and approximate location (via IP) to serve relevant ads. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. No catch data or personal information is shared with AdMob.

7. Data Storage

All catch data is stored locally on your device only. BrameStorm does not operate cloud servers, does not back up your data remotely, and cannot recover lost data. You are responsible for backing up your device.

8. Children's Privacy

Catch & Call is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any updated policy.
