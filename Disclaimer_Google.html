2. App Purpose

The App is designed to assist anglers in logging and tracking fish catches. Features include, but are not limited to:

  • Fun Day Mode: Log the size, species, date/time, and GPS location of catches.
  • Tournament Mode: Track top catches, assigned clip colors, and catch details including size, species, date/time, and GPS location.
  • Data Viewing & Export: View catches on a map within the app or export data (such as CSV or KML) for use on other platforms and mapping services.
