What Is Catch & Call?

Catch & Call is a fishing catch-logging app built for anglers who want fast, accurate records of every fish they catch — whether you are out for a fun day on the water or competing in a tournament.

Log your catches by tapping the screen or — with the Pro VC edition — speak your catch details completely hands-free using your Bluetooth headset. No phone touching required while you are on the water.

Three Editions — Pick What Fits You

🆓 Base Edition

Free with ads. Perfect for casual anglers.

  • Fun Day catch logging
  • Species tracking
  • Weight & length entry
  • No subscription required

📍 Tracker Edition

Monthly subscription. For anglers who want more.

  • Everything in Base
  • GPS catch locations
  • Map view of all catches
  • Tournament Mode
  • CSV & KML export
  • Voice Practice Mode

🎙️ Pro VC Edition

Monthly subscription. For serious tournament anglers.

  • Everything in Tracker
  • Full hands-free voice logging
  • Bluetooth headset support
  • Voice tournament mode
  • Voice Fun Day mode
  • Speak your catch — done

Key Features

  • 🐟 Log fish by species, weight, length, and clip color
  • 📍 GPS pin every catch location on a map (Tracker & Pro VC)
  • 🏆 Tournament Mode — track top 4 or top 5 catches with culling
  • 🎙️ Hands-free voice logging via Bluetooth headset (Pro VC)
  • 🗺️ View all your catch locations on an interactive map
  • 📊 Export your catch data as CSV or KML files
  • 🌍 Supports Canada, USA, UK, Australia and more
  • 📴 Works completely offline — no internet required to log catches

Who Is It For?

  • 🎣 Recreational anglers who want to track their catches over time
  • 🏆 Tournament competitors who need fast, accurate catch logging
  • 📍 Anglers who want to remember exactly where they caught each fish
  • 🎙️ Anglers who want to log catches without taking their hands off the rod
