1. Overview

Catch & Call is a fishing catch-logging and tournament-tracking app developed by BrameStorm, available on the Apple App Store for iPhone and iPad. This Privacy Policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and what is stored. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Data We Collect

Catch & Call collects only the data you directly enter into the app:

  • Fish species, size, weight, and catch date/time
  • GPS location coordinates (Tracker and Pro VC tiers only, when enabled by the user)
  • Clip colors and tournament round data (Tournament Mode)
  • App preferences and settings stored locally on your device

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or account registration information through the app
  • We do not transmit your catch data to BrameStorm servers
  • We do not sell your personal data
  • We do not share your catch logs with third parties

4. Location (GPS)

The Tracker and Pro VC subscription tiers may request access to your device location to record where fish were caught. Location data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to BrameStorm servers. You can deny or disable location access at any time through iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services.

The Free tier does not use GPS for catch logging. Google AdMob may use approximate location derived from network or IP-based signals for ad delivery. That processing is handled by Google and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Microphone & Speech Recognition

The Pro VC subscription tier includes voice control features that use your device microphone and Apple's on-device speech recognition framework (SFSpeechRecognizer). Voice audio is processed locally on your device by Apple's system services.

BrameStorm does not record, store, or transmit your voice audio. You can revoke microphone and speech recognition access at any time through iOS Settings → Privacy & Security.

6. Subscriptions & Purchases

Catch & Call offers auto-renewable subscriptions (Tracker and Pro VC tiers) processed entirely through Apple's App Store using StoreKit. BrameStorm does not collect or store your payment information. All billing, receipts, and subscription management are handled by Apple and are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

You can manage or cancel your subscriptions at any time through iOS Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions.

7. Advertising

The free tier of Catch & Call displays advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and approximate location information for ad delivery, fraud prevention, and related advertising operations. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Catch data entered into the app is stored locally on your device and is not shared with AdMob by BrameStorm. Subscribing to Tracker or Pro VC removes all advertisements.

8. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

In accordance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework, Catch & Call will request your permission before any advertising identifier (IDFA) is used for cross-app tracking purposes. You may change your tracking preference at any time through iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

9. Data Storage

All catch data is stored locally on your device. BrameStorm does not operate cloud backup, synchronization, or recovery services for this app. You are responsible for backing up your device and data using iCloud or iTunes.

10. Children's Privacy

Catch & Call is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
