### 2. App Purpose

The App is designed to assist anglers in logging and tracking fish catches. Features include, but are not limited to:

- **Fun Day Mode:** Log the size, species, date/time, and GPS location of catches.
- **Tournament Mode:** Track the top 4 or 5 catches, record clip colors assigned to each catch, and log the size, species, date/time, and GPS location.
- **Data Viewing & Export:** View logged catches on a map within the App or export data (CSV, KML) for use on other platforms and mapping services.
